The final game in the trilogy, Dark Souls 3 was the developers love letter to Dark Souls. It returned to the original setting of Lordran, with many maps and enemies from Dark Souls being reimagined for the new story.
It strived to bring together the best aspects of both Dark Souls (such as the map layout and healing mechanics) and Dark Souls 2 (such as weapon durabilities), while avoiding the causes for complaint from the resepctive games.
